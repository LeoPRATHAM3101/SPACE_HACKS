Hackathons

A hackathon is an event where people engage in rapid and collaborative engineering over a relatively short period of time such as 24 or 48 hours.

What is the purpose of Hackathron?

A hackathon, also known as a codefest, is a social coding event that brings computer programmers and other interested people together to improve upon or build a new software program. The word hackathon is a portmanteau of the words hacker, which means clever programmer, and marathon, an event marked by endurance.


About SpaceHacks


SpaceHacks is an exciting hackathon event where participants compete to create captivating galaxy and planet information websites. It's a unique opportunity for space enthusiasts and coding enthusiasts to come together and explore the wonders of the universe through code.

Whether you're a seasoned developer or just getting started, SpaceHacks offers an environment where creativity and innovation thrive. Our event is not just about coding; it's about expanding your horizons and reaching for the stars.

Join us for an unforgettable journey of coding, learning, and cosmic inspiration. Let's reach new heights together!
